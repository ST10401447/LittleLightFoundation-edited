About Us

Our Mission

At Little Light Foundation, our mission is to bring hope and help to underprivileged children and families through food, education, and support.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every child has the opportunity to thrive — where compassion lights the way for communities in need.

What We Do

We provide meals, educational support, mentorship programs, and emotional care to vulnerable children. Our dedicated volunteers and donors make this possible.
